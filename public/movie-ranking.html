Favourite movies of mine

10 movies I love watching.

  1. 3 Idiots

    2. A Bollywood blockbuster that combines humor and poignant social commentary, following the journey of three friends through the ups and downs of college life.

  2. Soorarai Pottru

    3. A critically acclaimed Tamil film inspired by the life of Captain Gopinath, depicting the challenges and triumphs of starting an airline in India.

  3. Jai Bhim

    4. A socially relevant Tamil movie shedding light on important issues, featuring compelling performances and a gripping storyline.

  4. 12th Fail

    5. A 2023 Indian Hindi-language biographical drama film based on the non-fiction book by Anurag Pathak. It portrays the inspiring journey of Manoj Kumar Sharma, who overcame extreme poverty to become an Indian Police Service officer.

  5. Dear Zindagi

    6. A thought-provoking Bollywood film featuring Shah Rukh Khan and Alia Bhatt, exploring life, relationships, and the pursuit of happiness through the eyes of a young woman.

  6. OK Kanmani

    7. A Tamil romantic drama film directed by Mani Ratnam, exploring modern relationships and the complexities of love in the contemporary world.

  7. Kiki's Delivery Service

    8. An enchanting animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki, following the adventures of a young witch named Kiki as she starts her own delivery service.

  8. Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

    9. A Bollywood coming-of-age film, exploring friendship, love, and self-discovery.

  9. Maara

    10. A captivating Tamil movie featuring the talented Madhavan. It's a feel-good movie and worth the watch.

  10. Kho Gaye Hum Kahan

    11. An intriguing movie that unfolds a tale of love, loss, and self-discovery. Three best friends juggle life as 20-somethings in Mumbai, where romance, ambition and heartbreak collide with the addictive draw of social media.
